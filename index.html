Survey Topic Key

This table is available to remind you of the statements that each topic relates to. This may help to guide your choices about how important the overall topic is to you (or other students), and how often you (or other students) discuss it with other students.
Short topic Long statement
Gender equality The government and companies should give priority to women when hiring for leadership positions.
Illegal immigration People who immigrated to the U.K. illegally, when caught, should be deported and sent back to their countries of origin.
Marriage traditions A wife should take her husband's last name once they are married.
Online sex work I would negatively judge a student who does online sex work (e.g. running an OnlyFans).
Israel-Palestine conflict Israel's previous activity in Gaza and the West Bank justify Hamas' actions.
Sexual assault and rape I think it is true that a person can be raped, even if they do not physically resist.
Trigger warnings The University of Nottingham should require professors to use trigger warnings in their classes.
Transgender participation in sports Transgender women (i.e., individuals who were assigned male at birth and transitioned later in life) should be allowed to participate in women’s sports.
Transgender bathroom use Transgender women (i.e., individuals who were assigned male at birth and transitioned later in life) should be allowed to use women’s bathrooms.
Views on politics I would end a friendship over a political disagreement.
Islam The Islamic religion is more likely than other religions to encourage violence amongst its believers.
The morality of killing Killing another person is never justified.
Gender roles A man should have the final word about decisions in his home.
Children and social media Children under 16 should be banned from having social media accounts.
Rules It is important to follow rules, even if the reason for the rule is not obvious.
Unions Trade and labour unions play a positive role in the modern-day United Kingdom.
The gender of political leaders On the whole, men make better political leaders than women do.
Smacking children It should be legal for parents to smack their children.
Scottish independence Scotland should become an independent country from the United Kingdom.
Homosexual couples as parents Homosexual couples are as good parents as other couples.
Views on race Many people nowadays are too sensitive about things to do with race.
Religion in politics It would be better for the United Kingdom if more people with strong religious beliefs held public office.
Religion and science Whenever science and religion conflict, religion is always right.
The death penalty The death penalty should be reintroduced for all cases of murder.
Shoplifting food It is sometimes acceptable to shoplift food if you cannot afford it.
Shoplifting clothes It is sometimes acceptable to shoplift clothes if you cannot afford them.
Import tariffs The United Kingdom should increase tariffs on foreign imports.
Climate change The national government should do more to fight global warming.
Cannabis legalisation Cannabis should be legalised in the United Kingdom.
Racial slurs in journalism It is acceptable for a journalist to use racial slurs when quoting something another person has said.
Use of cashless payments The United Kingdom should move to only allow cashless payments.
